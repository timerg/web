The International Summer School on Bio-inspired Systems and Prosthetic Devices is expected to be the first Asia-Pacific workshop on bio-inspired systems and prosthetic devices, analogous to the annual cognitive neuromorphic workshops in Telluride for the American region and in CapoCaccia for the European region. The summer school will be spearheaded by the Design Technology Center, National Tsing Hua University along with the Institute of Neuroinformatics, University of Zurich, Switzerland. The latter is the organizer of the annual cognitive neuromorphic workshop in Europe.

This summer school Aug. 25th-31th, 2015 at the Engineering School in National Dong Hua University, Taiwan. It will broaden the scope of the workshops further by providing :

  1. a systematic study of neuromorphic engineering through the principles of
    • computational neuroscience
    • neuroinformatics
    • neuromorphic devices and systems
    • bio-inspired architectures for biologically plausible neural computation
    • brain-machine interfaces
    • rehabilitation engineering
  2. detailed lecture sessions from the respective experts
  3. thought-provoking discussion forums among the speakers and participants
  4. hands-on projects for the participants
  5. project exhibitions from both academics and industry.
The program and the introduction of invited speakers are detailed in Workshop Program and General Information, respectively.

Registration information

The workshop is FREE for registration, but the seats are limited to 70 people. Therefore, all participants are suggested to complete the registration process to guarantee a seat and free lunch. To register for the workshop, you have to apply for an user account on the workshop website, login the website with your account, and then click the "registration" icon on the left to go through the registration process.
