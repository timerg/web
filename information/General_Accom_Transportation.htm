Accommodation Booking Service

While participants may book hotels by their own during the summer school, the BioPro2015 organizer provides the extra service of booking the National Dong Hwa University Guest House located within the NDHU campus. The discounted price is NTD 800 per night per person. Every two persons will share the same room with two double beds, and the breakfast is included in the room rate. The NDHU Guest house can accommodate a maximum number of 80 persons. First-come first-served policy is applied. The ¡§Accommodation¡¨ page details how to book the NDHU Guest House.
