1. For international participants who enter Taiwan through the Taoyuan Airport, you are suggested to follow the three steps below to reach the workshop venue:

    • 1. From Taoyuan Airport to Taipei Railway Station.(Bus, THSR, Taxi)
    • 2. From Taipei Railway Station to Hualien Railway Station.(Railway)
    • 3. From Hwalien Railway Station to Dong Hwa University.(Shuttle)

    1. From Taoyuan Airport to Taipei Railway Station

      You are able to reach Taipei Railway Station from Taoyuan Airport by taking

      • Bus
      • Taiwan High Speed Railway (THSR)
      • Taxi

      The maps below indicate the places to take the THSR (RED boxes), bus (YELLOW boxes), or taxi (GREEN boxes) at Terminal I and Terminal II. Taking a bus is more recommended because the bus goes directly from the TaoYuan Airport to the Taipei Station directly.

      You can follow the map of terminal I and terminal II to find the way to take Taiwan High Speed Railway, bus or taxi.

      • I. Arrival at Terminal I
      • II. Arrival at Terminal II
        • Bus:

          The Bus tickets can be purchased at the Ticket Counter of the Arrival Halls directly. The ticket price ranges between 90-140 TWDs, depending on the service provider. The pickup points for all buses are highlighted by the YELLOW boxes in the maps above.

          Terminal 1 bus pickup point: Bus pickup zone at B1 Arrivals.
          Terminal 2 bus pickup point: Bus pickup zone at the northeast arcade of the 1st floor Arrivals lobby.

          You could take the “1819 Bus” to Taipei Main Station and “1961 West Bus” to Taipei Railway Station.

        • Taiwan High Speed Railway:

          The UBUS shuttle service at the Arrival Halls takes passengers from the airport to the THSR TaoYuan station. One-way ticket costs 30 TWD, and the travelling time is about 25 minutes. The THSR ticket can be purchased at the station right before you get on the train, or you can book a seat online in advance. The on-line booking service is provided by the websites listed below, and the reservation can be made no more than 28days before your departure.

        • Taxi:

          Taxis Boarding Areas are highlighted by the GREEN boxes in the maps above. All taxis drivers must obtain a license from the Aviation Police Office to serve airport passengers. The Taxi fare is calculated by a certified meter automatically in the taxi. Do not take any unlicensed taxi or private car which does not use a meter to calculate the taxi fair. The taxi fair is estimated to be 1000~1200 TWDs, depending on the traffic conditions and the no. of red-light stops. If you do not speak Chinese, you can show the Chinese sentence below to the driver to go to the Taipei Railway Station:

          “請把這位貴賓送到台北火車站，謝謝。”

      • From Taipei Railway Station to Hualien Railway Station

        The timetable of the train and the ticket-booking service are provided by the websites below. Individual passengers can book and pay his tickets two weeks before his travelling date. Booked but unpaid tickets are only reserved till the midnight of the next day (counted from the date when the booking is made). Paid tickets can be reserved till the travelling date, and the tickets must be collected 30 minutes before the train departure at the ticket counter of any railway station. Your ID or Passport needs to be shown to the ticket counter for ticket collection.

      • From Hualien Railway Station to Dong Hwa University

        At Hualien Railway Station, you can take the 301 line shuttle to Dong Hwa University. As you walk out the Hualien Railway FRONT Station, you will see the shuttle station on your left-hand side. The Dong Hwa University Library is the final stop of the 301 shuttle.
