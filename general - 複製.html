Registraion Details

Procedure

To participate in the summer school, please apply for an account and sign in the BioPro2015 website first. After logging in the BioPro website, please refer to the Registration page.
Three steps for registration:

  1. In the questionnaire, please choose the answers in accordance with your identity and personal needs.
  2. By pressing the “submit” icon, two tables are shown
    • The top table requires you to fill in the information needed for the school organization (including your insurance during the event).
    • The bottom table summarizes total price (including the accommodation and train ticket service you choose).
  3. By pressing the “register” icon, a summary table comes out, and the payment method is shown below the table. The registration is completed after your payment is received (The payment status will become “Payment Finished”). Note that a delay of a few days might be needed for us to confirm the receipt of your payment.

Accommodation Booking Service

While participants may book hotels by their own during the summer school, the BioPro2015 organizer provides the extra service of booking the National Dong Hwa University Guest House located within the NDHU campus. The discounted price is NTD 800 per night per person. Every two persons will share the same room with two double beds, and the breakfast is included in the room rate. The NDHU Guest house can accommodate a maximum number of 80 persons. First-come first-served policy is applied. The "Accommodation page details how to book the NDHU Guest House.
