The iLMS@NTHU Account

After paying the registration fee, each participant will receive a confirmation email together with an iLMS account, starting with "biopro2015iXXX" and a password. The iLMS account is used to login the Integrated Learning Management system of the NTHU for

  1. Subscribing to hands-on projects of the BioPro Summer School.
  2. Initiating discussion forums.
  3. Sharing information or discussion for hands-on projects.

Registration and Extra Services

Registration Procedure

To participate in the summer school, please sign in the BioPro2015 website first. New users can apply for a web account simply by clicking “New User”. After logging in the BioPro website, please click the “Registration” icon to complete your registration by the following steps:.

  1. In the questionnaire, please choose the answers in accordance with your identity and personal needs.
  2. By pressing the “submit” icon, two tables are shown
    • The top table requires you to fill in the information needed for the school organization (including your insurance during the school).
    • The bottom table summarizes the total price (including the accommodation and train ticket services you choose).
  3. By pressing the “register” icon, a summary table comes out with the payment method shown below. The registration is completed after your payment is received (The payment status will become “Payment Finished”). Note that a delay of a few days might be needed for us to confirm the receipt of your payment.
  4. Before your payment is made, you are allowed to modify your registration package by clicking "Modify Registration Information".

Accommodation Booking Service

While participants may book hotels by their own during the summer school, the BioPro2015 organizer provides the extra service of booking the National Dong Hwa University Guest House located within the NDHU campus. The discounted price is NTD 800 per night per person. Every two persons will share the same room with two double beds, and the breakfast is included in the room rate. The NDHU Guest house can accommodate a maximum number of 80 persons. First-come first-served policy is applied. The "Accommodation" page details how to book the NDHU Guest House, and the table below summarizes the options for the booking service.It is possible to arrange larger rooms for domestic faculty and international participants who will travel with family members.
