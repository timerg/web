Room Information

The picture below show the rooms of the Dong Hua Guest House. All rooms are equipped with free Internet, air conditioning, desk, lamp, hair dryer, TV, refrigerator, and electric kettle, as well as amenities such as toothpaste and toothbrush, shower gel, shampoo, soap, comb, shower cap, tea-bag, shaving knife, cotton tips, paper slippers, facial tissue, and mineral water. Simple buffet-style breakfast is included in the room fee. Check-in time is 15:00, and check-out time is 12:00.

Room Reservation

Double rooms are reserved for BioPro2015 summer school participants. Two persons are assigned to each room, so please be prepared to share a room with another participant. For domestic faculty and international participants, it may be possible to book larger rooms for families traveling together. Please notify us on the "Transportation and Accommodation Booking Form" on iLMS system.

The room fee is 800 NTD per night per person, and is not included in the registration fee. Rooms must be booked for either the whole duration of the summer school (2015/8/24-2015/8/31) or half the duration of the summer school, ie, first half (2015/8/24-2015/8/27) or second half (2015/8/27-2015/8/31). Please login to the iLMS system and fill out the "Transportation and Accommodation Booking Form" so a room can be reserved.
